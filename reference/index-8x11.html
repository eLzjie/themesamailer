    Questions.

    What if my competitor claims the slot first?
    Your category is locked the moment you submit the form. We confirm within 1 business day. If someone else moves faster before we confirm, we'll tell you immediately — but it almost never happens on a first card.
    When does the first card mail?
    When all eight slots are claimed. We're not mailing a half-empty card. That's also why the launch price exists — to get the first card filled and prove the model.
    Can I see the homes you're mailing to?
    Yes. USPS publishes the EDDM route maps. We can walk you through exactly which streets are included in . Ask us on the call.
    What if my ad doesn't perform?
    Honestly, direct mail takes more than one send to build recognition. One card is a test. We'll tell you that upfront. We're not selling you a magic bullet — we're selling you the most affordable way to be in front of houses for 30 days.
    Do I have to commit to a year?
    No. Card # is a one-time purchase. If you want to renew for the next card, we'll reach out before it fills. No contracts, no auto-billing.
    Who designs the ad?
    We do, at no extra charge. You give us a logo, a phone number, and a sentence about what you want people to do. We send you a proof. You approve it. Done.
    Is this connected to that Valpak/Money Mailer thing?
    No. Valpak and Money Mailer are national franchise operations mailing to 20,000+ homes with 20+ competing advertisers. We're local, we mail to one specific neighborhood, and you're the only one in your category. Different thing entirely.
    What's the catch?
    The card doesn't exist yet. Card # only happens when all eight slots are claimed. That's the real answer. We're building this from zero, and we're being upfront about it.
